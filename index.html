Our Story

Built to Power the Future of Entertainment

TaraTech is the technology backbone of TáraDome Entertainment Group — a division engineered to build, manage, and innovate the digital infrastructure that makes world-class music and film possible.

Founded in Atlanta, Georgia, TáraDome Entertainment Group was conceived with a singular vision: to bring every creative sector of the entertainment world under one roof. From 1995, when Founder & CEO E. Kiszo Ugokwe operated his boutique fashion line, to 2004 when TáradomeFilms began producing and distributing motion pictures across Africa, to the 2021 launch of TáradomeMusik — the group has been methodically assembling the infrastructure of a global entertainment institution.

In 2006, Kiszo co-created the African Roving Film School in partnership with USC Film School and the Hollywood Film Institute, training the next generation of African filmmakers in script writing, production, and post-production. It is from this legacy of ownership, excellence, and technology that TaraTech was born — the engine that powers it all.

We leverage AI, data analytics, blockchain-based rights tracking, and proprietary platform development to empower creators, amplify African narratives, and deliver premium entertainment to the world.

The Visionary

E. Kiszo Ugokwe

Founder & CEO — TáraDome Entertainment Group

A child actor turned Electrical & Electronics Engineer with an MBA, Kiszo Ugokwe has spent three decades at the intersection of creativity and technology. He founded TáradomeFilms in 2004, producing and distributing motion pictures across Africa, and in 2006 co-created the African Roving Film School in partnership with USC and the Hollywood Film Institute Instructors.

His vision was always singular: build an institution — not just a company — where fashion, music, film, and technology converge under one roof. TáradomeMusik launched in 2021, and TaraTech followed as the technological backbone that makes it all possible.

Kiszo operates at the intersection of African creative excellence and global industry standards, building the infrastructure that ensures African creativity is not exported as raw talent, but owned, scaled, and institutionalized through African-led global enterprises.

The Elite Athlete is an ultra-premium performance management platform built for serious competitors.
